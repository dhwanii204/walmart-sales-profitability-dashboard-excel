# walmart-sales-profitability-dashboard-excel
## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes Walmart sales data using Microsoft Excel to identify revenue trends, profitability, regional performance, seasonal demand, and product performance.

The objective was not just to create a dashboard but to answer real-world business questions and provide actionable recommendations based on data.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Walmart management wants to understand:

- Which products generate the highest sales?
- Which products generate the highest profits?
- Which states contribute the most revenue?
- Which regions are operating at a loss?
- What seasonal sales trends exist?
- Which products should be promoted or reviewed?

## 🔍 Key Business Insights

- California contributes approximately **63%** of total sales.
- Washington is the second strongest performing state.
- Copier products generate the highest profits.
- Arizona and Colorado generate losses despite reasonable sales.
- December records the highest sales, while March achieves the highest profitability.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Continue investing in California and Washington.
- Review pricing and operational costs in Arizona and Colorado.
- Promote high-margin copier products.
- Reassess consistently loss-making products.
- Increase inventory before the year-end shopping season.
